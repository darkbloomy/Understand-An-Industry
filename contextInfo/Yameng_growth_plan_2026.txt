Project Proposal: Customer Intelligence Engine
Objective: 
Management has expressed a clear need for a more structured, data-driven understanding of customer feedback. We want to build an internal AI-powered tool that automatically analyzes and classifies open-text customer feedback. 
Business Owner: Elena Moujaes
Techincal Owner: Yameng Lu
Main Stakeholder: 
    • Business Development
    • Operations
    • CX (Customer Experience) Team
Divisions: PV, Heat, Energy, Enpal.pro
These stakeholders will use the output for performance analysis, prioritization of initiatives and management reporting.
The goal is to:
    • Convert unstructured feedback into structured data
    • Identify key pain points and positive drivers
    • Enable topic-based analysis over time
    • Allow team-level performance insights (e.g. Sales teams)
Process: 
    • The AI should automatically analyze open-text responses
    • Extract relevant topics / keywords mentioned by the customer
    • Tag each feedback entry with one or multiple standardized topics
    • detect sentiment per topic (e.g. Installation = positive, Service = negative)
Pain Point & Strength Analysis
For a selected time period:
    • What are the most frequently mentioned negative topics?
    • What are the most frequently mentioned positive topics?
    • What is the relative share of each topic?
    • Which topics are driving low CSAT / NPS?
Trend Analysis Over Time
    • How often a topic is mentioned over time (weekly / monthly)
    • Topic trends (e.g. “Service availability” mentions increasing?)
    • Sentiment trends per topic
    • Example: 
        ◦ Topic “Installation Quality” mentioned 120 times in January → 80 times in February
        ◦ Negative sentiment decreasing from 40% → 25%
Level: 
    • Per survey template
    • Across all survey templates
    • per Team or Individual (based on the custom data from AskNicely)
The output should:
    • Store topic tags as structured data fields
    • Allow filtering by: Topic, Sentiment, Survey template, Custom Data 
    • The AI should continuously learn / adapt
    • Manual override / correction option (if misclassified)
